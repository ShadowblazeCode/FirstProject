Character Facts/Other

Shadow; A girl with dark grey skin, black hair with turquoise stripes, and turquoise eyes. She is a shapeshifter and has the power to teleport 5-10 times a day. She is hardheaded but will listen to reason. She is overprotective of Silver, Enderflame, Aspen, Black Hole, and Aria

Silver Moon; A girl with light grey skin, silver hair with hot pink, light blue, and purple stripes, and purple eyes. She is a shapeshifter/unicorn hybrid with the power to summon fire with her hands and is fireproof. She is hardheaded and doesn't listen to anyone usually. Though she does protect Shadow with her life

Enderflame; A girl with light purple skin, dark blue hair with dark purple stripes, and dark purple eyes. She is Dracat (A cat with dragon wings and horns). She has the power to walk in others dreams. She is extremely shy and gets really nervous around shapeshifters

Aspen; A girl with white skin, light blue hair with white streaks, and light blue eyes. She is a pegasus and has the power to freeze things on contact. She is silly and unpredictable, plus she loves to randomly pull out basketballs

Black Hole; A boy with dark grey skin, black hair with orange streaks, and orange eyes. He is a unicorn and has the power to summon a light blue clear shield on command. He is smart and is the easiest to hang out with out of the Crew (Shadow, Silver, Enderflame, Aspen, and him)
